King Lear

Book Description





King Lear, after deciding to divide his kingdom between his three daughters, is decieved by the two daughters who claim their overwhelming love for him. King Lear spirals downward into a delerious state while learning what true love actually means through his honest daughter, Cordelia. Meanwhile, Glousceter's son, Edmund, feeds him lies about Glouscter's illegitamate son, Edgar, sending Glouscter into a similar situation as King Lear. The tragedy focuses on themes of madness, betrayal, chaos, justice, and love.
