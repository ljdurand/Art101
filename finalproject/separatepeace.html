A Separate Peace

Book Description





The novel takes place during World War II at Devon's Summer Session. The narrator, Gene, recounts his experience at the school and his friendship with Finny, a well-loved, funny jock attending school with him. While the war rages outside of the school, the boys face their own battles with jealousy and friendship, leaving some of their own battle scars.
